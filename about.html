Home

I am Matt Gauger. In addition to my blog, I wanted to share links, shorter thoughts, and write book reviews somewhere.

There's a line in Accelerando by Charles Stross that says the main character Manfred Macx:

… speed reads a new pop-philosophy tome while he brushes his teeth, then blogs his web throughput to a public annotation server

This is my own public annotation server. But beyond science fiction, I also realized that I am frequently sharing the same link to multiple people, and wanted to consolidate those shared links somewhere. I also wanted to track what books I was reading and potentially write mini-reviews of them. This serves as a public interface to my Remembrance Agent.

Note: Links to Amazon do not contain affiliate marketing links. All opinions are my own.
